    Who can become a MyDormot Agent?

  • An existing business owners with a shop or trading outlet such as a retail store, supermarket , pharmacy etc
  • The outlet must be in an unbanked or business location with high human traffic such as market, school, shopping malls, filling station etc
  • The outlet should make provision for at least a desk, chair, internet service and dedicated staff for this purpose.
  • Prospective agent should have at least working capital of N100,000 or more
  • Prospective MYDORMOT agent can register with either a business or personal name.
    • To become a MyDormot Agent, please fill the form HERE or visit any of our branches closest to you.

    Documents required to become a MyDormot Agent

    1. Valid means of Identification
    2. BVN
    3. One passport photograph
    4. Current Utility bill
    5. Good credit history
